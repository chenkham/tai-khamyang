About Tai-Khamyang

We Tai-Khamyang people are an indigenous community with a rich cultural heritage spanning centuries. Our language, traditions, and songs are precious treasures that connect us to our ancestors and guide us toward the future.

This digital platform will serves as a bridge between generations, helping preserve our language and culture for future Tai-Khamyang children and anyone interested in learning about our beautiful heritage.
